Baby Care for Mothers

Helping you nurture and care for your little one.

Essential Baby Care Tips

Feeding

Ensure your baby is breastfed or formula-fed every 2-3 hours. Watch for hunger cues like sucking on fingers.

Hygiene

Give regular baths with lukewarm water, and keep the diaper area clean to avoid rashes.

Sleep

Babies need 14-17 hours of sleep daily. Maintain a consistent sleep schedule and safe sleeping environment.

Bonding

Spend quality time holding, talking, and playing with your baby to foster a strong emotional connection.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I feed my newborn?

Newborns should be fed every 2-3 hours, whether breastfed or formula-fed.

How can I soothe a crying baby?

Check for basic needs like hunger, diaper change, or sleep. Rocking, swaddling, or a pacifier can also help.

What are signs of a healthy baby?

Steady weight gain, active movement, regular feeding, and wet diapers are all good signs.
