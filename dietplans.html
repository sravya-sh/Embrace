Diet Plan for Postpartum Depression

Boost your mood and health with a balanced diet.

Weekly Meal Plan

Breakfast

  • Oatmeal with fresh berries
  • Whole-grain toast with avocado
  • Greek yogurt with nuts

Lunch

  • Grilled salmon with quinoa
  • Spinach and chickpea salad
  • Brown rice with steamed vegetables

Snacks

  • Hummus with carrot sticks
  • Almonds and walnuts
  • Fruit smoothie

Dinner

  • Grilled chicken with sweet potato
  • Lentil soup with whole-grain bread
  • Stir-fried tofu with broccoli

Nutrition Tips

  • Include Omega-3 fatty acids (salmon, flaxseeds).
  • Consume plenty of fresh fruits and vegetables.
  • Stay hydrated and drink at least 8 glasses of water daily.
  • Limit processed and sugary foods.
  • Opt for whole grains over refined carbs.

For personalized plans, consult a dietitian or healthcare provider.
